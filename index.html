Here is a time line of Michael life:

  • August 29, 1958 - Michael Joseph Jackson was born in Gary, Indiana. He is the seventh of nine children.
  • 1963 - After several years of training, the Jackson 5 begin to perform in public.
  • December 14, 1969 - The Jackson 5 appear on "The Ed Sullivan Show."
  • 1970 - Her first album, "Diana Ross Presents the Jackson 5," includes the hits "I Want You Back" and "I'll Be There," which became number one.
  • 1972 - While still singing with The Jackson 5, Michael Jackson achieves his first solo album, "Got to Be There."
  • 1979 - Jackson's first adult album, "Off the Wall", is released. He becomes the first solo artist to place four singles on the top 10 chart.
  • 1982 - His album "Thriller" wins a record eight Grammy Awards and becomes the best-selling album of all time. In addition to the single "Thriller", it contains the songs "Billie Jean" and "Beat It."
  • 1984 - During the taping of a Pepsi-Cola commercial, Jackson suffers burns to his hair when his hair explodes in flames from an explosion.
  • 1985 - Jackson and Lionel Richie write what will become one of the best-selling singles in no time: "We Are the World." The song was produced to raise money for famine victims in Ethiopia. Jackson also pays $ 47.5 million for the rights to more than 250 songs written by John Lennon and Paul McCartney.
  • 1987 - His album "Bad" produces five number one singles and sells at least 22 million copies worldwide.
  • 1993 - Jackson reveals during a television interview that he inherited a disease that causes the pigmentation of his skin to disappear. He is also accused of sexually harassing a boy who often stayed at his mansion. The singer denies any type of abuse, reaches an agreement of allegedly 20 million dollars with the boy's family. No charges are ever brought against him.
  • 1994 - Jackson marries Lisa Marie Presley in the Dominican Republic. They divorce two years later.
  • 1995 - Releases the album "HIStory: Past, Present, and Future Book I." In August the song "You Are Not Alone" became the first single from pop music to hit the Billboard chart as a number.
  • 2002- Jackson shows his first son, Prince Michael II, when he is just a baby, from the balcony of a Berlin hotel to the thousands of admirers who watch from the street.
  • 2005- A judge finds Jackson not guilty of sexually harassing a 13-year-old cancer survivor at his Neverland mansion in 2003. He had been accused of getting him drunk and fondling, as well as behaving strangely and inappropriately with other children.
  • 2006- Plagued with financial problems since his trial, Jackson closes a 2,600-acre Neverland ranch home, laying off most of his employees after paying thousands of dollars in back wages to avoid a lawsuit by department officials. of work.
  • June 25, 2009 - Jackson dies at age 50, apparently of heart failure.
